Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

General Questions

1. What is the purpose of the Ethics Committee?

The Ethics Committee ensures that all research at IIT Delhi follows ethical standards and protects the rights and welfare of human and animal participants.

2. Do I need ethics approval for my research?

Yes, if your research involves human subjects or animals, you must obtain ethics approval before commencing your study.

3. How long does the review process take?

The review process typically takes 4-6 weeks, depending on the complexity of the proposal.

Submission Questions

4. How do I submit my research proposal?

You can submit your proposal using our online portal or via email at ethics@iitdelhi.ac.in with all required documents.

5. What documents do I need to submit?

You need to submit the completed ethics form, research protocol, informed consent form, and any other supporting documents.

6. Can I start my research before receiving approval?

No, you must obtain ethics approval before starting any research involving human or animal subjects.
