Institutional Animal Ethics Committee

The Institutional Animal Ethics Committee (IAEC) is responsible for ensuring the ethical use of animals in research and educational activities. Our primary focus is to safeguard the welfare of animals while promoting humane treatment.

Committee Structure

The IAEC is composed of members from various disciplines, including veterinarians, ethicists, and researchers, who work together to evaluate and oversee animal-related projects.

Guidelines and Policies

We adhere to national and international guidelines regarding the ethical use of animals, ensuring compliance with all necessary regulations.
